Computer Science Honors Society


Chambersburg Area Senior High School (CASHS)

CASHS Computer Science Honor Society supports a thriving environment for computer science interested students. Computer Science Honor Society encourages enthusiasm for computer science, honors academic excellence, and promotes service.







Joining


Eligibility & Expectations

To be eligible to complete an application, students should be in the 10th, 11th, or 12th grade, have attended CASHS for at least one year, have completed at least one year of either Advanced Placement Computer Science Principles or Advanced Placement Computer Science A or equivalent coursework, have earned at least 80%, B, in the course, and demonstrate service and leadership potential. Application availability and timeframe will be announced via morning announcements, society website, etc. After applications are reviewed, the students will receive an invitation. Only after the invitation is completed and dues are submitted will the student earn their membership. Students will participate in an induction ceremony, receive a pin, and a chord for graduation (as long as in good standing). Students are expected to participate in society projects/activities and complete community service (part of which is related to computer science) each year of membership.


Application

To apply for the CASHS Computer Science Honors Society, fill out an application (docx) or application (pdf) and email it to nichole.kunkle@casdonline.org.
